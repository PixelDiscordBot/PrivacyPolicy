Pixel Bot Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Pixel Bot Privacy Policy page. Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle and protect your data when using Pixel Bot.

Data Collection

We save some of the messages exchanged with Pixel Bot for quality and improvement purposes. This helps us enhance the functionality and user experience of Pixel Bot. We ensure that the data collected is handled securely and responsibly.

Data Use

The data collected is used solely for improving the quality of service provided by Pixel Bot and ensuring compliance with our terms of service. We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Appropriate measures are in place to safeguard and protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Requesting Data Takedown

If you wish to request the takedown of your information or have any privacy concerns, please join our support server: Support Server. Our team will address your concerns as promptly as possible.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
